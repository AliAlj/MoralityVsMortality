Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Morality Vs Mortality handles information. Morality Vs Mortality is a single-player narrative mystery game.

Information We Collect

Morality Vs Mortality does not collect personal information from users. The app does not require an account, email address, phone number, location, camera access, microphone access, or payment information.

Local Game Data

The app may save gameplay information locally on your device so you can continue your progress. This may include your entered player name, selected detective character, current act, collected evidence, unlocked dialogue, searched areas, analysis results, and game completion status.

This information is stored locally on your device and is not sent to us or to any third party.

Analytics and Tracking

Morality Vs Mortality does not use analytics tools, advertising trackers, or third-party tracking services.

Internet Connection

Morality Vs Mortality does not require an internet connection for gameplay. The app does not transmit gameplay data to external servers.

Children’s Privacy

Morality Vs Mortality does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect or transmit personal information, no personal data is shared with us.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete locally saved game data by using the in-game reset or new game option, or by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
